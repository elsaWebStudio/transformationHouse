Changing lives through spiritual growth.

Life After Addiction

Our goal, our most heart felt hope is to help a recovering addict and see him begin to function in society again as a useful, spiritually minded person. We encourage brotherhood and fellowshiop amongst our clients and have seen this change lives and open hearts time and again. If you or a loved one would like more information on our program or services offered please contact us.

About the Homes

Transformation Project Homes is an addiction recovery program located in Watson Chapel, AR. We are a subsidiary of Centennial Fellowship Church. We offer space for up to 7 clients at any one time. Our program is based on spiritual healing, through spiritual growth, we strive to provide a clean, healthy, spiritual based lifestyle for our clients. We want to offer a secure, peaceful enviroment for our clients to integrate them back into society with a strong moral and spiritual foundation. Through mentoring, counseling and service with Centennial Fellowship Church we are achieving these goals daily.

On-Site Living

We are located at 5705 Sorrells Road, in Watson Chapel, AR. We are currently a male only facility, with hopes of opening a womens home soon. Our houses are set-up for 2 people per bedroom, with in-house laundry and kitchens. We encourage our clients to find work and offer transportation to find jobs, and meet any appointmens they may have. There is an on-site manager to help clients on a daily basis.
